Compares buying an item with ex vs. selling it back to ex via the official Currency Exchange. A scan is just 2 Trade2 calls (all buy legs + all sell legs) and results cache for 2 min — repeat scans reuse the cache unless you tick Force live refresh. Results are estimates; whisper/stock can change before you execute both legs.
