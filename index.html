Tab Tracker

        
Values the currency parked in a stash tab at live market prices and ranks it by what's worth selling — items with live buyers sit on top with their real sell value; ones with no buyers drop to the bottom as no live buyers. Make tab(s) public and price every item at a marker divine value — this reads it via Trade2, no login needed. Trade2 caps each price at 100 results, so spread big sets across several marker prices (e.g. 11, 12, 13, 14).

      

      

        
        
        
        
        
Enter your account name and load your tracked tab.
